How to care for plants

Take care of plants with all your hearts

Adjust Lighting

When caring for indoor plants, make sure the
lighting is right for the plant.

Don't water too often

Watering ornamental plants indoor does
not have to be done every day.

Fertilize regularly

The nutrients most indoor houseplants need are
nitrogen for balance and potassium for stem strength

Maintain Temperature

When caring for indoor plants, make sure the
room temperature is neither too hot nor too cold.
